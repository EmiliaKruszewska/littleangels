  • Home Care
    • We are here to offer a helping hand. Wheter it's doing the dishes, the laundry or jus getting dinner on the table, our care workers can be there to help. We can also bathing, dressing and change bed linens or do laundry.
  • Continue Care
    • We offer both Line-In Care and 24 Hour home Care.
  • Skilled Caregivers
    • Each one of our proffessional care workers has been carefully selected based on their qualifications and their dedication to home care. Their specialist skills will not only give elderly people all the assistance tey need, but also help to build meaningful companionship.
  • Home Health
    • Little Angels LLC. provides personal care and helping around the house to help older adults remain independent and living in their homes.
  • Assisted Living
    • A lack of mobility or ill health can be an obstacle for erlerly people when it comes to their personal health and hygiene. Little Angels LLC. offer help wherever it's needed. Our care workers provide essential support from getting dressed to going to the bathroom. They also manage and remind taking medications, plan and prepare meals and monitor diet and eating habits. Our caregivers can do shopping or drive to appointments.
  • Independent Living
    • Little Angels LLC. believes that a person's passion for living comes from the people around them. Our care workers are experienced in supporting both the mental and emotional wellbeing of elderly poeople and their families. A core focus of home care is to build a meaningful relationship that can encourage socialising, happiness and independence.
